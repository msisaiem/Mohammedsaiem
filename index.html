Executive Biography

Mohammed Saiem Islam is a multidisciplinary professional with over 18 years of experience spanning engineering, business development, digital technology, and administrative leadership. His career reflects a unique blend of technical expertise, entrepreneurial thinking, and a passion for continuous self-development. Saiem began his journey into technology at an early age in 2002, driven by curiosity and self-learning. He initially focused on graphic design, mastering tools such as Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, while also developing foundational skills in HTML and web technologies. This early exposure laid the groundwork for his future in both creative and technical fields. He later earned a Diploma in Computer Science and Engineering in Dhaka, Bangladesh, further strengthening his technical capabilities. Expanding his expertise into industrial design, he pursued studies in shipbuilding at the Bangladesh Marine Academy, followed by professional training in AutoCAD for shipbuilding and construction design through an Autodesk-authorized institute. In 2008, Saiem advanced his education internationally in Singapore, where he specialized in mechanical and shipbuilding structural design at the Institute of Technical Education. Alongside his formal education, he completed multiple professional and trade certifications, enhancing his versatility across engineering disciplines. Professionally, Saiem began his career in Singapore in 2007 as an Office Administrator. Over time, he expanded his role across multiple engineering companies within the marine sector, gaining extensive experience in project coordination, operations management, and business development. He has played a key role in supporting the growth and establishment of several construction firms and service-based e-commerce businesses, contributing both strategically and operationally. Beyond his professional achievements, Saiem is an entrepreneur and innovator, currently involved in developing digital solutions such as Drivelaar, an app designed to simplify compliance and services for TLC drivers, and Laaref Technology, which focuses on affordable digital solutions for small and medium-sized businesses. Known for his calm confidence, adaptability, and forward-thinking mindset, Saiem thrives in environments that require problem-solving, leadership, and innovation. His diverse background allows him to bridge the gap between engineering, technology, and business. Outside of work, he enjoys traveling and exploring new environments, embracing adventure and continuous discovery. He has a strong appreciation for nature and village life, valuing simplicity, fresh air, and meaningful time with family. He is also a fan of horror and action films, reflecting his interest in dynamic and engaging storytelling. With a lifelong commitment to learning and growth, Mohammed Saiem Islam continues to pursue opportunities that combine technology, business, and real-world impact. He is also the co-founder and head of business development of Drivelaar and Laaref Technology.

Today, Mohammed Saiem Islam bridge the gap between heavy engineering and digital innovation, leading initiatives like Drivelaar and Laaref Technology.
