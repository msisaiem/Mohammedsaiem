Mohammed Saiem Islam

Professional Biography

Executive Overview

Mohammed Saiem Islam is a multidisciplinary professional with over 18 years of experience spanning engineering, business development, digital technology, and administrative leadership. His career reflects a unique blend of technical expertise, entrepreneurial thinking, and a passion for continuous self-development.

Early Tech & Creative Roots

Saiem began his journey into technology at an early age in 2002, driven by curiosity and self-learning. He initially focused on graphic design, mastering tools such as Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, while also developing foundational skills in HTML and web technologies. This early exposure laid the groundwork for his future in both creative and technical fields.

Education & Technical Foundation

He later earned a Diploma in Computer Science and Engineering in Dhaka, Bangladesh, further strengthening his technical capabilities. Expanding his expertise into industrial design, he pursued studies in shipbuilding at the Bangladesh Marine Academy, followed by professional training in AutoCAD for shipbuilding and construction design through an Autodesk-authorized institute.

In 2008, Saiem advanced his education internationally in Singapore, where he specialized in mechanical and shipbuilding structural design at the Institute of Technical Education. Alongside his formal education, he completed multiple professional and trade certifications, enhancing his versatility across engineering disciplines.

Professional Career & Marine Sector

Professionally, Saiem began his career in Singapore in 2007 as an Office Administrator. Over time, he expanded his role across multiple engineering companies within the marine sector, gaining extensive experience in project coordination, operations management, and business development.

Entrepreneurship & Innovation

Beyond his professional achievements, Saiem is an entrepreneur and innovator. He is the Co-founder and Head of Business Development of Drivelaar and Laaref Technology. His work focuses on simplifying compliance for drivers and providing affordable digital solutions for businesses.

Personal Philosophy & Interests

Known for his calm confidence and adaptability, Saiem thrives in environments that require problem-solving and leadership. Outside of work, he enjoys traveling, exploring nature, and is a fan of horror and action films.

With a lifelong commitment to learning, Mohammed Saiem Islam continues to pursue opportunities that combine technology, business, and real-world impact.
